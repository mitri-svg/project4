A café is a type of restaurant which typically serves coffee and tea, in addition to light refreshments such as baked goods or snacks. The term "café" comes from the French word meaning "coffee".

Modal title

According to the range of products sold, they are divided into: coffee shop , cafe- bakery ( confectionery ), ice cream parlor , grill cafe , cafe- bar , Internet cafe and cafeteria ; By location: stationary and street. Cafes can be located in separate buildings , but more often, unlike most restaurants, these are premises inside the building (usually on the first floors) or extensions to them. Another type of cafe is roadside . More often they are located in separate buildings near federal or local roads. Seasonal cafes are also common - near the seashore or river in certain months, usually during the warm period (but, for example, at ski resorts, on the contrary, in the winter). Often near cafe buildings there is an outdoor cafe with outdoor tables and chairs. In countries with temperate climates , they operate during the warm season.

According to the most plausible version, the world's first coffee shop called “Maktab al-irfan” (“Circle of Thinkers”) was opened in Istanbul in 1554 by two Syrian merchants [2] . There is a version that gives priority to the first cafe in Europe to the establishment (“kafana”), opened in Belgrade in 1522 on lower Dorčol ( Serbian: Dorћol ), after the capture of the city by the Turks [3] . The establishment served only coffee, hookahs and pipes. In America, the first coffee shop was opened in 1670 in Boston by William Penn [4] . In Europe ( Austria ) - Kulchitsky's (Ko(a)lechitsky's) coffee house in Vienna , founded after the Viennese victory in 1683 [5] . The first coffee house on the territory of the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth was founded in Warsaw in the vicinity of the Saxon Garden in 1724 , during the reign of Augustus the Strong [6] .

On January 1, 2016, to replace the old GOST R 50762-2007, the new GOST 30389-2013 “Catering services. Catering establishments. Classification and general requirements” [1] defines the difference between cafes and other public catering establishments: restaurants, bars , canteens , snack bars , fast food establishments, buffets , cafeterias , coffee shops, culinary stores.
